### âš ï¸ **Common Warning Signs**

| Warning Sign | What It Means | Recommended Action |
|--------------|---------------|-------------------|

| Feedback is consistently vague | Wrong participants or poor questions | Review stakeholder selection, improve facilitation |

| Teams resist using outputs | Poor translation to actionable insights | Add synthesis step, clarify next steps |
