## ğŸ”„ Step 4: Implement and Iterate

### ğŸ“Š **Implementation Process**

<details>
<summary><strong>ğŸ¯ Running Your First Tool Session</strong></summary>

**Pre-Session (1-2 weeks before):**
1. **Download and review** the tool's PDF worksheet
2. **Customize materials** for your specific context
3. **Send calendar invites** with clear agenda and objectives
4. **Share worksheets** with participants in advance (if appropriate)
5. **Prepare logistics** (room, tech, materials)
6. **Brief facilitators** on their roles

**During Session:**
1. **Set context** - Explain purpose and expected outcomes
2. **Follow the tool structure** - Use the worksheet as a guide
3. **Capture outputs** - Document decisions, insights, and action items
4. **Manage time** - Stay on schedule while allowing discussion
5. **Summarize** - Review key takeaways and next steps

**Post-Session (within 48 hours):**
1. **Send summary** - Key decisions, action items, owners
2. **Share artifacts** - Photos of whiteboards, completed templates
3. **Collect feedback** - What worked, what could be improved
4. **Plan follow-up** - Schedule next touchpoints
5. **Document learnings** - Update your co-design process documentation
