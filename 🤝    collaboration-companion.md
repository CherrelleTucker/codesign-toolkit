<details>

- **Primary Concern**: "Will this make my job easier?"
- **Engagement Style**: Hands-on, practical, detail-oriented
- **Value**: Workflow efficiency, accuracy, reliability
- **Best Touchpoints**: User testing, prototype reviews, training sessions

- **Primary Concern**: "Will this deliver measurable impact?"
- **Engagement Style**: Strategic, outcome-focused, time-constrained
- **Value**: ROI, mission alignment, organizational benefits
- **Best Touchpoints**: Executive briefings, success story presentations

**Intermediaries** (People who support or influence adoption)
- **Primary Concern**: "How do I help others succeed with this?"
- **Engagement Style**: Collaborative, supportive, network-focused
- **Value**: Training resources, peer support, implementation guidance
- **Best Touchpoints**: Train-the-trainer sessions, champion networks

**Technical Staff** (People who integrate and maintain solutions)
- **Primary Concern**: "How does this fit our infrastructure?"
- **Engagement Style**: Detail-oriented, security-focused, systematic
- **Value**: Documentation, technical support, compatibility
- **Best Touchpoints**: Technical reviews, integration planning, troubleshooting
