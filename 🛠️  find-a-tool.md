*"Who are our users and what do they really need?"*

<details>
<summary><strong>ğŸ¯ Phase Overview</strong></summary>

**Goal**: Identify core needs, relevant actors, and entry points through participatory, inclusive engagement.

**Key Questions**:
- Who are the end users and what are their roles?
- What problems are they trying to solve?
